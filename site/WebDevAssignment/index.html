Synopsis Of Ready Player One

About The Movie

"Ready Player One" is a Fictional movie that is centered around the idea of virtual reality.The main character of this movie Wade Watts, an orphan teenager who loves to play this virtual game called "The OASIS", it was created by late James Halladay. Before James passed away he creates a quest/treasure hunt in the game. And whoever find the items hidden will have access to a great fortune left behind by James Halladay Himself. Wade, the main character of this story, having a username of "parzival" wins the game along with his friends Art3mis, Aech, and Shoto.However, the process of winning this challenge was a tough one, as they have to continually battle agains the I0I who have evil motives of taking over the game.All things work in favour of parzival and his friends and they emerge victorious.


Another important this to watch out for in the movie is the amazing love between parzival and one of his allies in the game. Although only meeting eachother through the virtual world their relationship seems to grow when they meet in person on a quest to battle the I0I and avoid chaos in the OASIS.
